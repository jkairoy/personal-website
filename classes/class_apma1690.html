APMA1690
APMA1690 covers several topics that use computers to solve challenging problems in probability and statistics. The course focuses on using computers to develop intuitions about classical analytic results in probability and statistics. The class covers pseudorandom number generation, stochastic approximation (simulation), random walks, Markov chains and MCMC, graphical models, and dimension reduction.
