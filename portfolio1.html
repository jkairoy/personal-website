THE PROJECT
The challenge of this project was to design and create a website to preview Optec LLCs' new Sentinel device and service. This platform aimed to reduce the downtime of production and research ablation machines by using a microcomputer to communicate between a computer, the ablation device, and the Sentinel cloud notification service. In order to launch their product, Optec needed a new website and specifically requested an animated graphic to communicate the function of their service to prospective customers.
