THE PROJECT
Been There is a project created by myself and a friend and is based on the idea that oftentimes, the best comfort to a trauma survivor can be knowing that they are not alone in their trauma. Been There enables anyone, specifically trauma survivors to share stories of hardships and to recieve one-way statements of reassurance in the form of an "I've been there", or a personal message. Been There uses a tagging system to show willing participants stories like their own in the hopes that they find a community to lean on. The project can be found here.
